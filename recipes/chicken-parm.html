Chicken Parm

Chicken Parm is an italian american staple dish!

Pound chicken to an even thickness, don't skip on the seasoning, add parmesan to the bread crumbs, rest before cooking, go easy on the sauce, and make sure your over is very hot!

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Parmesean
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Bread Crumbs

Steps:

  1. Pound chicken to an even thickness
  2. don't skip on the seasoning
  3. add parmesan to the bread crumbs
  4. rest before cooking
  5. go easy on the sauce
  6. make sure your over is very hot
